Nutrient Calculator

The nutrient calculator

  • shows food nutrient contents, and compares them to the recommended intake for a group of people
  • lists nutrient and recommended intake values for 100g or 3.527oz of food items,
  • calculates price / nutrient value ratios for complex products and combinations of complex products (several day long plans),
Start planning on the calculator page.
